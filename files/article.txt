Lenovo is a leading global technology company known for its innovative personal computers, smartphones, and IT solutions, with a rich history dating back to 1984.
Company Overview
Founded: Lenovo was established on November 1, 1984, in Beijing, China, originally as Legend by a group of engineers led by Liu Chuanzhi. The company initially focused on distributing foreign IT products before transitioning to manufacturing computers.
Headquarters: Lenovo has its global headquarters in Beijing, China, and Morrisville, North Carolina, USA. It operates research centers in various locations, including China, Germany, and Japan.
